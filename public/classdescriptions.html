CLASS DESCRIPTIONS



After Burn

Add variety to your training - no more boring cardio! Scientific studies have shown interval training promotes the highest caloric burn. This class will take you through weight training interspersed with fun moves designed to elevate your heart rate. Bring water and a towel!

Butts and Guts

Get ready for Summer! This 45 minute class targets the lower half of the body utilizing ankle weights. Tone your thighs, glutes and abs. This class is ideal for anyone experiencing an upper body injury.

Conditioning Circuit

Interval training with small group rotating stations.

Pilates

Pilates is a body conditioning routine developed by Joseph Pilates that strengthens the core and creates long, lean muscles. Benefits include flat abdominals, increased circulation, flexibility, and balance, decreased back/neck pain and toned thighs and buttocks. Pilates also promotes weight loss. Although practiced by dancers and gymnasts, many Hollywood actors and exercise enthusiasts practice Pilates to promote their overall health and well being. This classical Pilates mat class emphasizes learning the basic Pilates exercises. Emphasis is placed on pelvic and spinal alignment and the proper use of the breath. Benefits include increased core strength, flexibility and posture.

Pilates/Barre Fusion

Pilates/Barre Fusion is a fluid blend of Pilates, Booty Barre and two short cardio bursts built into the workout to get your sweat on: the perfect balance of strength, flexibility and cardio. This class is about looking and feeling great.

Piloxing

Piloxing is a low impact, fat burning workout that combines the three wonderful disciplines of ballet, Pilates and boxing. 1/2 pound weighted gloves are worn to increase the calorie burn (typically between 600-900 calories) and tone the arms. High energy music is played to motivate you during this class! Piloxing uniquely blends the power, speed and agility of boxing with the beautiful sculpting and flexibility of Pilates. It is a fat torching, muscle sculpting, core-centric interval workout, guaranteed to whip you into shape. PILOXING is the brainchild of Swedish dancer and celebrity trainer Viveca Jensen.

Plyoga

We are so lucky to have this class at Kickfit! Plyoga is the latest Tabata based fitness class to introduce itself to the Fitness Industry and it is really catching on! PLYOGA is a perfect blend of PLYO/cardio to get the heart rate up intertwined with yoga poses (chair, warrior one, tree) to bring the heart rate down while increasing strength and flexibility. If you are tired of your workout, this is the class for you. It is different and FUN! No equipment needed-just you and a towel.

Power Walk

Join us for a fast paced, energizing, full- hour Power Walk around Fair Haven. Wear your Piloxing gloves for an extra challenge. A great way to burn calories while enjoying fresh air and even better conversation! Class is complimentary for passbook holders and annual members.

Tabata Interval

This is a HIIT (high intensity interval training) class. Tabata training involves getting the heart rate up with box jumps, jumping jacks,etc for a short period of time followed by training with weights for a short period. Maximum calorie burn. You WILL get your sweat on and see great results!

Total Body Conditioning

A total body conditioning workout starting at the top of the body and working through all the muscle groups on the way down! Enhances muscular strength an endurance.

Total Body Pilates

By popular demand! Get your workout done early and be ready to conquer whatever the world had to throw your way! This class is a fusion of Total Body Conditioning (40 minutes) and Pilates (20 minutes).
